What Cookies Are (and Similar Technologies)

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners. We also use similar technologies such as local storage and session storage to store information on your device.

Cookies can be "persistent" (remain on your device until deleted or expired) or "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser).

Why We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as setting privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms. These cookies enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Examples include:

  • Authentication cookies to keep you logged in
  • Security cookies to protect against fraud
  • Session cookies to maintain your preferences during your visit

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use this information to improve our website and services.

We will not run analytics cookies until you choose to accept them. You can manage your preferences at any time through the cookie preferences control on our website or in your Account Settings.

Examples include:

  • Google Analytics cookies to track page views and user interactions
  • Performance monitoring cookies to identify and fix technical issues

What We Do Not Do

We do not:

  • Use cookies to track you across other websites
  • Share cookie data with third parties for advertising purposes
  • Use cookies to create detailed profiles of your browsing behavior
  • Require cookies for basic website functionality (except essential cookies)

Third-Party Cookies and Services

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control these cookies. These third parties may use cookies to collect information about your online activities across different websites.

Third-party services we use include:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how visitors use our website. You can opt out of Google Analytics by visiting Google Analytics Opt-out.
  • Stripe: Processes payments and may set cookies for fraud prevention and security purposes.

For more information about how these third parties use cookies, please visit their respective privacy policies.

Your Choices and Controls

Cookie Banner and Preference Controls

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies (including analytics)
  • Reject non-essential cookies (only essential cookies will be used)
  • Manage your preferences (choose which types of cookies to allow)

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:

  • Clicking the "Cookie Preferences" link in the footer of any page
  • Visiting your Account Settings page and clicking "Cookie Preferences"
  • Visiting this Cookie Policy page

Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or delete certain cookies. However, if you choose to disable cookies, some parts of our website may not function properly.

To learn more about how to manage cookies in your browser, visit:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites you visit that you do not want to have your online activity tracked. There is no universal standard for how DNT signals should be interpreted, and our website does not currently respond to DNT signals. However, you can control cookie preferences through the methods described above.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
